Musician

Tsz Kiu is a composer and improviser based in Melbourne. Having completed an Honours degree in composition at the University of Tasmania, he is currently studying electrical engineering, aiming to incorporate computer and electronic geeky stuff into his music.

Tsz Kiu was born and raised in Hong Kong (not China). He came to Australia to study music in 2012, majoring in classical piano performance in undergraduate and composition in honours. He was exposed to music of a variety of genres such as jazz, contemporary classical music and world music. Tsz Kiu was influenced by the music of Elliott Carter, Iannis Xenakis, Gérard Grisey, Marc Hannaford. Near the end of his music degree, Tsz Kiu started to be interested in electroacoustic and computer music. Tsz Kiu then decided to study electrical engineering, aiming to connect his own music to science and mathematics.

Tsz Kiu is currently interested in a variety of subjects, including computer improvisation and algorithmic composition.
