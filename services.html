Services

Composition Lessons (100 AUD per hour)

I offer composition lessons in a very unique way. Being trained in classical piano and composition at a university level, I have acquired a sophisticated taste in music, as well as strong knowledge in both classical and jazz music theory. I am also utilising my skills in engineering and programming in my composition practice. Having tutored programming at a university level for two years, I can teach you how to program in Python (a general-purpose programming language). And being an active contributor of the algorithmic composition software Abjad, I can teach you how to utilise Python, LilyPond (which means you don't have to pay for Sibelius or Finale ever again!) and Abjad into your composition.

I am offering individual lessons over Zoom anywhere in the world, and in-person lessons around Melbourne, Victoria (with travel cost).
